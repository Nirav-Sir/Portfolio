About Me

I am Dr. Nirav Pareshkumar Mehta, an Assistant Professor at Parul University with a passion for academic excellence and research innovation. With a diverse educational background including B.C.A., M.Sc. (I.T & C.A), L.L.B., and Ph.D. in Computer Science, I bring a multidisciplinary approach to my teaching and research.

As an IQAC Coordinator and MIS Coordinator at Parul University, I am committed to maintaining high standards of quality in education and efficient management of information systems. Additionally, as an Advanced AI Trainer and AI/ML Based Automation Developer, I specialize in developing cutting-edge artificial intelligence solutions and training professionals in advanced AI technologies. My research interests span across blockchain technology, artificial intelligence, machine learning, data analytics, and healthcare informatics.

I have authored 5 books on various computer science topics and published multiple research papers in prestigious journals. My contributions have been recognized through awards and certifications from various national and international organizations.

Beyond academics, I am passionate about sports and have served as a cricket coach, leading teams to championship victories. I believe in holistic development and strive to inspire my students to excel in both academic and extracurricular pursuits.
